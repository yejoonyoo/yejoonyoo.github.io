Hi! I'm Yejoon Yoo

My research interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction, with a focus on LLM-powered conversational agents and immersive simulations that support personalized learning experiences.

Previously, I worked at RippleAI, Softberry, and NeoLAB Convergence.
